Muriel and Alexander met in a seance. After that Muriel would go to private session to meet with him.
The only other person in the room was the medium. There in order to translate the words of the ghost.
The book The Five Lieutenants mentions that Alexander's love-making became increasingly passionate.

That on itself doesn't seem that odd. The more they get to know each other the more they are willing to explore.
What is odd is not even that one is attracted to ghosts. Spectrophilia is not scientifically justified, but some quote Succubus and Incubus as evidence of it in folklore.
What is difficult to imagine, for me at least, is being the medium for a session of intense love-making.
